Our Hour of Code Event

On Friday (03/11/2023), we and our team (Team 35) Principles of Information Systems (SITE 1101, CRN10406) visited one of the local schools in Baku, Kaspi Lyceum Branch of the White City, as part of the Hour of Code event for students at this school. Members of our team are Sardar Ziyatkhanov, Malahat Hajiyeva, Teymur Aliyev. We conducted a lesson according to the instructions for our homework. Sardar Ziyatkhanov actively participated in the planning process and during the event itself was quite active and successfully completed the assigned tasks. He also participated in writing a detailed report and wrote a short report and edited videos. Malahat Hajiyeva was active during the entire event. With the help of Malahat, we agreed with the school and successfully held this event. Also Malahat was very active during the writing reports. Teymur Aliyev was Active during the event. He successfully completed all assigned tasks. He also helped with reports. The first thing after discussing with the school administration about holding this kind of event was that we discussed it among ourselves and divided our responsibilities. We were allowed to teach a lesson in 6B and 6C classes. The two classrooms were connected so that each student had access to a computer. The first thing the teacher did was introduce us to the class and we started presenting. We acted strictly according to the plan that we discussed.
